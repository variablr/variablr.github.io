About

Variablr is one of my creative outlets. These images come together using an iPhone 5s, Afterlight, Cut Me Out, and Glitch Wizard whenever I have a moment of free time (usually on the throne). I like to combine geometric shapes, landscapes, textures, and whatever happens to catch my eye. The images always come as a surprise. I'll edit an image on and off throughout the day or week until I get something that really surprises me. I'm really happy that technology provides this medium of expression. Feel free to reach out via email or check out some of my other work on Instagram. Thanks! Charlie Nickell
